## **Application Description**

The **Carbon Emission Calculator** is a mobile app designed to help users calculate their personal carbon emissions from travel, which allows them to input their travel data across various categories. By breaking down emissions into detailed categories, users can understand their environmental impact and make more informed, sustainable travel choices.

### Key features:
- **Travel Categories & Emissions Calculator**: Users can input travel data for various transport modes (e.g., buses, cars, trains) and calculate their carbon emissions using standard formulas.
- **Flight Emissions**: Special calculation for air travel, considering factors like distance and class.
- **User Interface & Customization**: An intuitive design that allows easy data input, result display, and the option for users to enter personalized emission factors for more accurate results.
- **Sustainability Tips & History Tracking**: Provides actionable tips to reduce carbon footprints and allows users to track their emissions over time.
